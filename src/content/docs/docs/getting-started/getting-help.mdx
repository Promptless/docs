Have questions or need assistance? We're here to help.

### HOW WE CAN HELP

Our team is happy to assist you with:

- **Trigger events** — Questions about how Promptless processed specific events, unexpected behavior, or troubleshooting issues with different trigger types
- **Setup and configuration** — Help configuring integrations, triggers, context sources, or doc collections
- **Guiding the agent** — Tips for steering Promptless to produce better documentation suggestions, setting up behavior guidance, or fine-tuning outputs
- **General questions** — Anything else related to Promptless

Don't hesitate to reach out. We genuinely enjoy helping teams get the most out of Promptless.
