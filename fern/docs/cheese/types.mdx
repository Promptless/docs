# Types of Cheese

There are many different types of cheese, each with its own unique characteristics. Here's a quick overview of some popular varieties.

## Hard Cheeses

Hard cheeses are aged for longer periods, resulting in a firm texture and intense flavor.

- **Parmesan** - A sharp, nutty Italian cheese perfect for grating
- **Cheddar** - A versatile cheese ranging from mild to extra sharp

## Soft Cheeses

Soft cheeses have a creamy, spreadable texture and milder flavors.

- **Brie** - A French cheese with a soft, edible rind
- **Camembert** - Similar to brie but with a deeper, earthier flavor
- **Cream Cheese** - A mild, spreadable cheese popular in baking

## Blue Cheeses

Blue cheeses feature distinctive blue or green veins of mold throughout.

- **Gorgonzola** - An Italian blue cheese with a creamy texture
- **Roquefort** - A tangy French blue made from sheep's milk
- **Stilton** - An English blue cheese with a rich, complex flavor
