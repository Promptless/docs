# Introduction to Cheese

Welcome to the wonderful world of cheese! This page serves as your gateway to understanding one of humanity's oldest and most beloved foods.

## What is Cheese?

Cheese is a dairy product made from the curdling of milk. It comes in hundreds of varieties, each with unique flavors, textures, and aging processes.

## Why Cheese Matters

Cheese has been a staple of human cuisine for thousands of years. From the sharp tang of aged cheddar to the creamy smoothness of brie, there's a cheese for every palate.
